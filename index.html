How to Capture a Perfect 360° Image

  1. Use a tripod or steady surface - Keep your phone at the same height
  2. Stand in one spot - Rotate your body, don't move your feet
  3. Capture 8-12 photos - Take photos every 30-45 degrees
  4. Overlap photos by 25-30% - Ensure each photo overlaps with the previous
  5. Consistent lighting - Avoid changing light conditions
  6. Keep horizon level - Try to keep the camera at the same angle

Note: This tool uses OpenCV.js for professional-grade image stitching. The first time may take longer to load the library.
